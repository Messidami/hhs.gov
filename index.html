Make Our Children Healthy Again & Rebuild the Standard of Living

The Presidential Commission to Make America Healthy Again (MAHA),And Rebuild The Standard of Living, has released a groundbreaking assessment identifying key drivers behind the childhood chronic disease crisis. The report exposes a range of contributing factors—including poor diet, accumulation of environmental toxins, insufficient physical activity, chronic stress, and overmedicalization.
