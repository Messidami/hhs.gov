HHS Small Business Support

Empowering Entrepreneurs

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is committed to empowering small businesses that contribute to health, wellness, and community growth. Through financial resources, guidance, and contract opportunities, HHS ensures that entrepreneurs have the support they need to succeed.

Available Support Programs

Grants & Funding

Financial assistance for startups and small businesses working in health-related fields.

Training & Mentorship

Access workshops, mentorship programs, and professional networks to grow your business.

Contract Opportunities

Guidance on securing federal contracts within HHS and related sectors.

Technical Assistance

Resources to strengthen operations, compliance, and long-term sustainability.

Success Stories

Small businesses across the nation have grown with the help of HHS programs. From healthcare startups to wellness-focused entrepreneurs, our support creates lasting impact in communities.
