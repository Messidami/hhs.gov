About HHS

U.S. Department of Health & Human Services

Who We Are

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is the United States government's principal agency for protecting the health of all Americans and providing essential human services. Our mission is to enhance the well-being of all Americans by providing effective health and human services and fostering advances in medicine, public health, and social services.

Our Mission

To provide the building blocks that Americans need to live healthy, successful lives. We aim to protect the health of all Americans and provide essential services, especially for those who are least able to help themselves.

What We Do

  • Provide health insurance through Medicare and Medicaid
  • Support medical research through the NIH
  • Ensure food and drug safety through the FDA
  • Combat public health threats and disease outbreaks
  • Fund and support state and local health initiatives

Core Values

Accountability, transparency, service, equity, and innovation are the guiding principles that help us serve the public better.

\"Improving the health, safety, and well-being of America.\"
