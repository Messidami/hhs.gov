Programs & Services

Explore the wide range of public health and support programs offered by HHS.

Medicare & Medicaid

Providing health coverage to millions of Americans.

HHS administers Medicare and Medicaid to offer affordable and reliable health insurance options to seniors, low-income families, and people with disabilities.

Public Health Services

Protecting the nation from health threats.

Through the CDC and other agencies, HHS monitors disease outbreaks, provides immunizations, and runs health education campaigns nationwide.

Child & Family Support

Helping children and families thrive.

Programs include Head Start, child welfare services, adoption and foster care support, and assistance for low-income households.
